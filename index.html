About the Game:

  • There is a secret five letter word chosen
  • Players have six guesses to figure out the secret word. After six guesses, they lose (Word masters heading turns red)
  • Only valid words will be considered as guesses
  • Use backspace to delete the last character and press Enter to validate the five letter word.
  • Enter will only work if the five letter word typed is valid.
  • If the player guesses a letter that is in the right place, it is shown as green
  • If the player guesses a letter that is in the word but not in the right place, it is shown as yellow
  • It does account for however many of the letter exist in the word. For example, if the player guesses "SPOOL" and the word is "OVERT", one "O" is shown as yellow and the second one is not.
  • If the player guesses the right word, the player wins (rainbow animation is shown in the wordmasters title)and the game is over.
